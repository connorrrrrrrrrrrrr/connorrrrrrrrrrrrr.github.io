About Us

We're two local high school friends from Bellevue who are dedicated to providing premium mobile auto detailing services that combine quality, consistency, and attention to detail. This isn't just a hobby or side hustle, this is our company that we take pride in, and hope continues to grow. We aren't like big businesses. We make communication our priority and are committed to listening to our customers, because nothing would be possible without you. We love hanging out and enjoy creating a result that we can be proud of. We hope you join us on this journey of creating quality results that leave us prideful of our work, and you prideful of your ride.

Frequently Asked Questions
What areas do you serve?

We bring our detailing services directly to your home for your convenience. Our service area is up to 10 miles around the city of Bellevue, WA. We do exceptions so if you're outside that area, book anyway and we'll do our best to make it work!

How often should I get a detail?

The frequency depends on your needs, some clients only need one detail every few months, but many choose to have one every month to maintain their vehicle's premium appearance

What are the differences between packages?

Our packages are designed to tailor your needs, such that clients can choose to only book interior or exterior services. Our Full Detail Packages include a thorough cleaning inside and out. To see more details about what each package offers, view our Services

How long does it take?

The time depends on what package or addons the client chooses. Typically interior jobs can take between 2-3 hours, exterior jobs 1-3 hours, and full detail 2-5 hours

What do I do with my coupon?

If you have a coupon from a door hanger or referral card, just present it to us at checkout (after we finish your detail) and we'll apply the discount! If someone has used YOUR DISTRIBUTED referral card, your coupon will automatically be applied the next time you checkout with us.
